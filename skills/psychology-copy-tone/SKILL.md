# Write calm, professional marketing content

## Use when
- Drafting hero text, service descriptions, FAQs, or CTA copy
- Improving tone across landing page sections
- Editing content for clarity and trust

## Goal
Write short, reassuring, human copy that feels professional and gentle.

## Steps
1. Keep the message clear and emotionally safe.
2. Use short sentences and plain language.
3. Focus on trust, clarity, and welcome.
4. Avoid hype, pressure, or overly salesy language.
5. Make the copy easy to scan on mobile.

## Rules
- Do not overpromise outcomes.
- Avoid jargon unless the audience clearly expects it.
- Keep CTAs soft and respectful.
- Match the existing tone across the page.

## Good qualities
- Calm
- Supportive
- Clear
- Professional
- Readable

## Verify
- Read the section aloud.
- Check that the copy fits the visual layout and does not feel crowded.
